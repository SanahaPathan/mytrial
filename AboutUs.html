About Back to School

A pioneer in ECCE (Early Childhood Care and Education), we are the largest preschool chain in Asia. With an incredible network of more than 1900+ centers in over 750+ cities, we are committed to spearheading child development across the nation. Having touched the lives of more than 9,00,000 children in India, BackToSchool, a decade on, remains focussed on nurturing the ‘unique potential’ in every child.

With years of dedicated research, BackToSchool has set unparalleled standards in the CDE (Child Development & Education) space. Having acknowledged the uniqueness of each child and their infinite potential. BackToSchool has is its proprietary pedagogy, ‘iLLUME’, India’s one and only University-verified preschool curriculum.
